About Us

A symbol of unity, compassion, and resilience.

Rise for Relief represents the strength of communities united after devastating typhoons in the Philippines. Through compassion-led support, volunteers and contributors come together to help affected families rebuild their lives, restore dignity, and transform disaster into resilience.

Our Purpose

Vision

To create a future where communities affected by natural disasters emerge stronger, inspired by compassion-led support and united by humanitarian cooperation.

Mission

To provide compassionate, impactful, and sustainable relief to disaster-stricken communities, helping them rebuild resilience, dignity, and hope.
